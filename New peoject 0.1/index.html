“Filipino Values in the Digital Age”

‘How social media and influencers are shaping the Filipino youth’

Traditional Filipino Values

🤝Bayanihan - Unity & helping others

❤️Malasakit - Compassion, care for others

🙏Paggalang - Respect for elders, authority, and culture

😁Pakikisama - Maintaining Harmony & Good Relationships with Peers

Social Media Culture Values

💎Materialism - Focus on brands, wealth, aesthetics

📸Personal Branding - “Curated self-image,” followers, likes

🛒Consumerism - Following fast trends, viral products

⚡Instant Gratification - Chasing likes, shares & viral trends

What if?

What if our country’s social media platform banned all content promoting toxic lifestyles? Would Filipino youth go back to bayanihan, pakikisama, malasakit, and paggalang or create new ways of expressing identity online?
